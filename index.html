FAQ

Common questions before the first call

What happens after I request an automation assessment?

Polaris reviews your current tax process, goals, tools, and pain points, then recommends a focused scope for assessment, roadmap, or implementation support.

Do we need an in-house tax team to work with Polaris?

No. Polaris is specifically designed to help businesses that do not have an in-house tax function, as well as teams that already have tax resources but need modernization support.

Do you replace our CPA firm or tax preparer?

No. Polaris can work alongside your existing advisors by improving the process, data, documentation, and handoffs that make advisory and compliance work more efficient.

What kinds of tools can you help with?

Polaris can help evaluate and design workflows using spreadsheets, databases, workflow platforms, AI tools, document automation, tax software integrations, and reporting dashboards.

What if we are not sure automation is appropriate?

That is a good reason to start small. Polaris can talk through example workflows with your team so you can understand practical use cases before deciding whether a broader automation project makes sense.
